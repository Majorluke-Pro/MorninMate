1. Overview

MorninMate is a smart alarm and morning routine app. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using MorninMate, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for account authentication and identity
  • Password — stored securely and hashed by our authentication provider (Supabase); we never see your plaintext password

2.2 Profile Information

During onboarding and through app use, we store:

  • Display name
  • Preferred wake time
  • Morning difficulty rating (self-reported)
  • Favorite wake-up game (Math, Memory, or Reaction)
  • Wake-up goal (a personal motivation note)
  • Profile icon / avatar selection
  • Gamification stats: XP points, level, streak count, demerit count

2.3 Alarm Data

For each alarm you create, we store the alarm label, time, repeat days, and sound/pulse settings.

2.4 Wake Session Data

Each time an alarm fires, we record whether you successfully completed the wake-up game (success or failed). This powers your streak and XP tracking.

2.5 Device Permissions

  • Notifications: We request permission to send local notifications to trigger your alarms. Notification data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2.6 On-Device Storage

We store your profile icon preference in your device's local storage for quick access between sessions.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating the app.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase (supabase.com), a cloud database and authentication platform. Supabase encrypts data in transit (TLS) and at rest. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) policies so that each user can only access their own data.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all profile data, alarms, and wake session records are permanently removed.

7. Children's Privacy

MorninMate is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data
