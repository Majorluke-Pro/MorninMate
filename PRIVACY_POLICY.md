## 1. Overview

MorninMate is a smart alarm and morning routine app. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. By using MorninMate, you agree to the practices described here.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** — used for account authentication and identity
- **Password** — stored securely and hashed by our authentication provider (Supabase); we never see your plaintext password

### 2.2 Profile Information
During onboarding and through app use, we store:
- **Display name**
- **Preferred wake time**
- **Morning difficulty rating** (self-reported)
- **Favorite wake-up game** (Math, Memory, or Reaction)
- **Wake-up goal** (a personal motivation note)
- **Profile icon / avatar selection**
- **Gamification stats**: XP points, level, streak count, demerit count

### 2.3 Alarm Data
For each alarm you create, we store:
- Alarm label
- Alarm time
- Repeat days (days of the week)
- Sound / pulse settings

### 2.4 Wake Session Data
Each time an alarm fires, we record whether you successfully completed the wake-up game (status: success or failed). This powers your streak and XP tracking.

### 2.5 Device Permissions
- **Notifications**: We request permission to send local notifications to trigger your alarms. Notification data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

### 2.6 On-Device Storage
We store your profile icon preference in your device's local storage for quick access between sessions.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---|---|
| Authenticate your account | Email, password hash |
| Personalize your experience | Name, wake goal, avatar |
| Schedule and fire alarms | Alarm times, days, sound settings |
| Track your progress | XP, streak, demerit, wake session history |
| Display your stats | Wake session success/failure counts |

We do **not** use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it for any purpose beyond operating the app.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on **Supabase** (supabase.com), a cloud database and authentication platform. Supabase encrypts data in transit (TLS) and at rest. Authentication tokens are managed securely per Supabase's platform standards.

We implement Row Level Security (RLS) policies so that each user can only access their own data.
